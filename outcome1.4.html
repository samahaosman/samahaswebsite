1.4 Describe the risks that might impact digital projects – what causes digital projects to fail? Why?

When designing a digital project there are many bumps that can lead to project failure for example, beginning a project a few months later apple releases a new version of IOS and website you have developed doesn’t work probably anymore. Or mid project the company merges with another company and the concept of their market strategy is completely different. Or mid project a key member of the team leaves your company for another opportunity. or few months doing the project, a competitor releases a new website with new and better feature that will draw your customers away. Also, likelihood that you will go over-budget, over time and inconsistent delivery .
